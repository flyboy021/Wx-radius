Wx Radius — Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 21, 2026

Wx Radius is an aviation weather app that shows METARs, TAFs, ATIS, and weather radar for airports near you. This policy explains what data the app uses and how. In short: we don't collect, store, sell, or share your personal information, and there are no accounts, ads, or trackers.

Location

If you allow it, the app uses your device's location to center the weather search on where you are. Your location is used on your device to build a map area, and only the resulting coordinates are sent to the weather data provider to retrieve nearby weather. We do not store your location, associate it with you, or track your movements. You can decline location access and search by airport identifier instead.

Information we do not collect

  • No account, name, email, or password.
  • No analytics, advertising identifiers, or third-party trackers.
  • No data is sold or shared for marketing.

Third-party services the app contacts

To show weather and maps, the app requests data from the following services. Your use of the app is also subject to their privacy practices:

  • FAA Aviation Weather Center (aviationweather.gov) — METAR/TAF data, reached through a Cloudflare Worker proxy operated by the developer.
  • RainViewer — weather radar tiles.
  • CARTO — base map tiles.
  • clowd.io / atis.info — digital ATIS text.
  • jsDelivr — software libraries and map border data.

These requests include standard technical information (such as your IP address and the map area requested) that is necessary to deliver the data. We do not control how these providers handle that information.

Tips

The optional "Tip the dev" button opens an external Buy Me a Coffee page in your browser. Any payment is handled entirely by Buy Me a Coffee under their own terms and privacy policy; the app does not receive your payment details.

Children

Wx Radius is a tool for pilots and is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
